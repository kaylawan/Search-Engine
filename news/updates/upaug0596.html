August 5, 1996
DOLE FOR PRESIDENT UNVEILS NEW WEB SITE

 

Digital Dole For President 
Volume 3, Number 1
August 5, 1996 

To the many new members of our mailing list, welcome!

On August 1, Dole for President unveiled the first individually 
customizable political web site. If you haven't been to the new site, 
here's a rundown:

When you first visit the site, you are given the option of setting up a 
custom Dole web page. (Requires a Netscape 2.0 compatible 
browser) Each custom page contains a personal tool bar with your 
own "In Box" that contains any new materials added to the site since 
your last visit. The toolbar also highlights the issues you told us 
were important to you and offers a home-state icon for information 
specific about the Clinton record and the Dole agenda in your state.

We've redesigned the main topics as well:
About Bob Dole - with an updated story about Bob Dole and a new 
Elizabeth Dole story.

Dole Interactive - all new! Make your own buttons and posters. 
Check out our new crossword puzzle and trivia quiz. Download a 
background for your computer and send a custom postcard to your 
friends.

The Dole Agenda - Submit your opinion on each of Dole's stands 
and learn more about the Clinton record and the Dole agenda in your 
state.

Get Involved - With our new "Link to DFP Online" program, 
simply copy a line of text to your web site and we do the rest. The 
appropriate graphic and link pops up on your web page.

On The Campaign Trail - Use our interactive map to see where 
we've been in your state.

Newsroom - Keep track of the campaign's Press Releases, 
Speeches, Updates, and Hot Topics

Find it all at:

http://www.dolekemp96.org

We'll keep in touch with your during the campaign, we hope you'll 
visit back and keep up with us.
