FIRST TIME OR FREQUENT VISITOR - WHAT YOU CAN FIND AT THE DOLE/KEMP WEB SITE

 

Digital Dole/Kemp '96
Volume 3, Number 6
October 11, 1996 

To our many thousands of new subscribers, welcome!

Whether you are a first time visitor or a frequent guest who 
hasn't had a chance to look around recently, we want to let 
you know what you can find at the Dole/Kemp web site:

Customization - http://www.dolekemp96.org/
If you are using a Netscape 2.0 compatible browser, you can 
customize our site to your interests. Tell us what issues are 
important to you, what state you are from, and we'll make a 
web site just for you. You'll get your own personal tool bar 
and in box. Each time you visit back, the site checks when 
you last visited and puts everything added since then into 
your personal in box.

About The Team - http://www.dolekemp96.org/about/
Here you can learn more about Bob Dole and Jack Kemp 
and their families. Read the very personal story of Bob 
Dole's life - from childhood in Russell, Kansas to World 
War II, his injury and recovery, to his service for America in 
Congress.

Dole Interactive - http://www.dolekemp96.org/interactive/
A place for some fun. Take a trivia quiz, fill out a crossword 
puzzle, send a postcard to your friends, or make your own 
button or poster. Download wallpaper for your computer 
and show your support for Dole/Kemp.

The Dole Agenda - http://www.dolekemp96.org/agenda/
Learn Bob Dole and Jack Kemp's agenda for America's 
future. A comprehensive listing of Bob Dole's stand on 
issues, a state by state analysis of Bill Clinton's liberal 
record in your state and Bob Dole's agenda for a better 
America in the Dole/Kemp Economic plan and Crime and 
Drug plan.

Get Involved - http://www.dolekemp96.org/involved/
Here you can volunteer, donate, and find out how to contact 
the campaign in your area. Place the Dole/Kemp Logo on 
your personal web page! Each time someone views your 
page, you can show your support for Dole/Kemp. Visit our 
site, choose the graphic you want on your page, place the 
appropriate line of text on your web page and that's it. Are 
you a Business Entrepreneur? Be sure to join the Dole/Kemp 
Committee for Economic growth. 

On The Campaign Trail - http://www.dolekemp96.org/trail/
This is where you will find our Debate and Convention 
coverage. Track our campaign across the country - a listing 
of the cities and towns the campaign has visited in the course 
of outlining their agenda for a better America.

News Room - http://www.dolekemp96.org/news/
All the latest commercials, speeches, press releases, Hot 
Topics, and other multimedia files.

Thanks again for your continued support, and we hope to 
see you again soon.
